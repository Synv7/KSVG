Our Mission

KSVG 103.5 is an independent, community-driven radio station delivering bold music curation, local voices, and live shows with personality. We support independent artists, local events, and authentic radio experiences.

What We Play

Eclectic mix: alternative, indie, punk, electronic, and deep cuts you won't hear on corporate stations. Curated shows from local DJs and special weekly features.
